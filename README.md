## 🕉️ About

This portfolio is a devotional web experience inspired by **Mahakal** — Lord Shiva in his form as the Lord of Time and Death. Every pixel, animation, and interaction is designed to evoke the mystical, cosmic energy of Shiva — from the sacred Om chanting in the background to the trishul-adorned UI elements.

> _"यतो वा इमानि भूतानि जायन्ते, येन जातानि जीवन्ति, यत् प्रयन्त्यभिसंविशन्ति।"_
>
> _"From whom all beings are born, by whom they live, and into whom they return."_
>
> — Taittiriya Upanishad 3.1

## 📿 Sacred Mantras of Lord Shiva

### 🔱 Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra (The Great Death-Conquering Mantra)

> **ॐ त्र्यम्बकं यजामहे सुगन्धिं पुष्टिवर्धनम्।**
> **उर्वारुकमिव बन्धनान् मृत्योर्मुक्षीय मामृतात्॥**
>
> _Oṁ tryambakaṁ yajāmahe sugandhiṁ puṣṭi-vardhanam_
> _Urvārukam iva bandhanān mṛtyor mukṣīya māmṛtāt_
>
> **Meaning:** We worship the three-eyed Lord Shiva, who is fragrant and nourishes all beings. May He liberate us from the bondage of death, just as a ripe cucumber is freed from its vine, and grant us immortality.

---

### 🔱 Panchakshari Mantra (The Five-Syllable Mantra)

> **ॐ नमः शिवाय**
>
> _Oṁ Namaḥ Śivāya_
>
> **Meaning:** I bow to Lord Shiva — the auspicious one, the supreme consciousness. These five sacred syllables (Na-Maḥ-Śi-Vā-Ya) represent Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Ether.

---

### 🔱 Rudra Gayatri Mantra

> **ॐ तत्पुरुषाय विद्महे महादेवाय धीमहि।**
> **तन्नो रुद्रः प्रचोदयात्॥**
>
> _Oṁ Tatpuruṣāya Vidmahe Mahādevāya Dhīmahi_
> _Tanno Rudraḥ Pracodayāt_
>
> **Meaning:** We meditate upon the Supreme Being, Mahadeva. May Lord Rudra illuminate and inspire our intellect.

---

### 🔱 Shiva Tandava Stotram (Opening Verse)

> **जटाटवीगलज्जलप्रवाहपावितस्थले**
> **गलेऽवलम्ब्य लम्बितां भुजङ्गतुङ्गमालिकाम्।**
> **डमड्डमड्डमड्डमन्निनादवड्डमर्वयं**
> **चकार चण्डताण्डवं तनोतु नः शिवः शिवम्॥**
>
> _Jaṭāṭavī-galaj-jala-pravāha-pāvita-sthale_
> _Gale'valambya lambitāṁ bhujaṅga-tuṅga-mālikām_
> _Ḍamaḍ-ḍamaḍ-ḍamaḍ-ḍaman-nināda-vaḍḍamarvayaṁ_
> _Cakāra caṇḍa-tāṇḍavaṁ tanotu naḥ Śivaḥ śivam_
>
> **Meaning:** With the holy waters of the Ganga flowing from his matted locks, wearing a garland of mighty serpents around his neck, and to the thundering rhythm of the damaru — Shiva performs his fierce cosmic dance (Tandava). May He bestow auspiciousness upon us.

---

### 🔱 Lingashtakam (First Verse)

> **ब्रह्ममुरारिसुरार्चितलिङ्गं निर्मलभासितशोभितलिङ्गम्।**
> **जन्मजदुःखविनाशकलिङ्गं तत्प्रणमामि सदाशिवलिङ्गम्॥**
>
> _Brahma-murāri-surārcita-liṅgaṁ nirmala-bhāsita-śobhita-liṅgam_
> _Janmaja-duḥkha-vināśaka-liṅgaṁ tat praṇamāmi sadā-śiva-liṅgam_
>
> **Meaning:** I bow before the Shiva Linga, which is worshipped by Brahma, Vishnu, and all the gods, which shines with a spotless radiance, and which destroys the sorrows born of the cycle of birth and death.

---

### 🔱 Shiva Dhyana Mantra

> **ध्यायेन्नित्यं महेशं रजतगिरिनिभं चारुचन्द्रावतंसं**
> **रत्नाकल्पोज्ज्वलाङ्गं परशुमृगवराभीतिहस्तं प्रसन्नम्।**
> **पद्मासीनं समन्तात् स्तुतममरगणैर्व्याघ्रकृत्तिं वसानं**
> **विश्वाद्यं विश्ववन्द्यं निखिलभयहरं पञ्चवक्त्रं त्रिनेत्रम्॥**
>
> _Dhyāyen nityaṁ Maheśaṁ rajata-giri-nibhaṁ cāru-candrāvataṁsaṁ_
> _Ratnākalp-ojjvalāṅgaṁ paraśu-mṛga-varābhīti-hastaṁ prasannam_
> _Padmāsīnaṁ samantāt stutam amara-gaṇair vyāghra-kṛttiṁ vasānaṁ_
> _Viśvādyaṁ viśva-vandyaṁ nikhila-bhaya-haraṁ pañca-vaktraṁ trinetram_
>
> **Meaning:** One should always meditate upon Mahesha (Shiva), who is radiant like a silver mountain, adorned with the beautiful crescent moon, whose body glows with jeweled ornaments, who holds an axe, deer, boon, and protection in his hands, who is serene, seated in the lotus posture, praised by all the gods, clad in tiger skin — the origin of the universe, revered by all worlds, the destroyer of all fears, five-faced and three-eyed.

## 🚀 Getting Started

```bash
# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/Dutta2005/Portfolio-v2.git
cd Portfolio-v2

# Install dependencies
npm install

# Start development server
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:5173](http://localhost:5173) to witness the divine experience 🔱
